## When to Recommend Each Chain

- **Ethereum**: Large swaps (>$10k) where deep liquidity matters
- **Arbitrum**: Good balance of liquidity and low gas, ideal for most users
- **Base**: Cheapest gas, growing liquidity, good for smaller amounts
- **BNB Chain**: Low gas, strong BSC-native token support
- **Polygon**: Very cheap, good for frequent small transactions
